Tour Du Mont Blanc

Erik and, fellow photographer, Anthony attempt to hike around the the grand and snow-capped Mont Blanc, in the Alps. You can watch their progress on their Youtube channel: Night Owl Productions.

While this hike served as a personal test of endurance and fitness, it's greater appeal lay in the incredible views and memories that Erik and Anthony were priveledged to experience. Sleeping next to herds of ibex, forging friendships with countless people from around the world, and resting in the shadow of tallest mountain in western Europe... This was truly the journey of a lifetime.

Photography

Nature photography is Erik's greatest passion, easily eclipsing all other intrests and hobbies. Please enjoy a small glimpse into his portfolio.

Triassic Cuddle Animation

Rooted in his love of all animals, Erik developed a strong interest in paleontology in college. He aims to help educate others about prehistoric life though short hand-drawn animations, and eventually documentary-style video essays that explore the relationships between different branches on the tree of life.

Z-Scan

Erik and his colleague showcase an innovative drone detection device developed by Ian Annase, fouder of Zing Drone Solutions. Able to locate the drone operator of any drone within it's detection radius, Z-Scan is a valuable tool that prisons can utilize to improve their security.

The video highlights how prison staff react to watching a Z-Scan demonstration in their facility in Pendleton, Oregon.
